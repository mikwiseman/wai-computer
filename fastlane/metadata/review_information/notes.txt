Test account credentials:
Use the current App Review credentials supplied directly in App Store Connect.
Do not store review account passwords in this repository.

Steps to access the app:
1. Launch the app
2. Enter the App Review email and password on the login screen
3. Tap "Login"
4. The app opens to the main recording screen

To test recording:
- iOS: Tap the record button and speak
- macOS: Go to Settings > Dictation > enable it, then use the hotkey (Right Command by default)

Account deletion:
- Sign in with the test account
- Open Settings
- Use Delete Account in the account section and confirm deletion

macOS App Store review notes:
- The Developer ID distribution path is documented separately from App Store review metadata.
