WaiComputer turns your voice into organized knowledge. Record meetings, conversations, lectures, and personal thoughts — get live transcripts, AI summaries, action items, and a searchable second brain.

YOUR AI SECOND BRAIN
Everything you record is transcribed, summarized, and indexed. Ask Wai questions across all your recordings and get answers with direct references back to the source.

RECORD ANYTHING
One tap to start, one tap to stop. Live transcription appears as you speak in 100+ languages. Works offline — audio is captured locally first and uploaded as soon as you have a connection.

AI SUMMARIES + ACTION ITEMS
Each recording gets an automatic summary with key points, decisions, and extracted action items. Regenerate any summary with one tap if you want a fresh take.

SEARCH YOUR MEMORY
Three search modes (Hybrid, Semantic, Text) across every transcript. Find the exact moment something was said and jump straight to that segment.

ASK WAI ANYTHING
Talk to your second brain with the built-in Companion chat. Get answers grounded in your recordings, with clickable citations to the underlying audio.

SPEAKER IDENTIFICATION
Assign names to voices once and Wai recognizes them in future recordings. Manage your roster of speakers in Settings.

ORGANIZE WITH FOLDERS
Group recordings into folders. Star important ones. Move things to Trash and restore them later — nothing is lost by accident.

IMPORT AUDIO FILES
Already have audio you want to transcribe? Pick any mp3, wav, m4a, ogg, opus, or flac file and Wai will transcribe and summarize it.

GUEST MODE
Try the app without signing up. Recordings stay on your device until you sign in — at which point they migrate to your account automatically.

PRIVACY FIRST
Recordings are encrypted in transit and at rest. We never log raw transcripts, emails, or recording titles. You can delete your account permanently from inside the app at any time.

KEY FEATURES
- Live transcription with three speech-to-text providers
- Auto-generated summaries, key points, decisions, action items
- Hybrid / semantic / full-text search
- Speaker identification with voiceprints
- Companion AI chat grounded in your recordings
- Folder organization, starring, trash with restore
- Audio file import (mp3 / wav / m4a / ogg / opus / flac)
- Foreground-service recording survives background, screen-off, and incoming calls
- Magic-link sign-in (no password required)
- Guest mode with auto-migration after sign-in
- Russian + English UI
